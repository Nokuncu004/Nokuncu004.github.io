General Info.

Toastmasters is a global nonprofit organization that provides leadership and communication skills training to individuals from all walks of life. In addition, trained members offer support and guidance for people looking to improve their public speaking skills through participating in club meetings..

.

The youth programme aims to empower young ones so that they are able to articulate themselves better and it aims to build their confidence.

Benefits are that it allows for confidence and improved communication skills.

It offers benefits like improved communication, public speaking and leadership skills through a structured curriculum and a supportive environment. It also teaches punctuality.

Founded in 1924 by Dr. Ralph C. Smedley in Santa Ana, California, Toastmasters began as a small gathering aimed at helping individuals hone their speaking skills. Over the years, it grew into a global movement, empowering millions of members to become confident communicators and leaders.
